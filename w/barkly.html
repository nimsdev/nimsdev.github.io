barkly

Barkly is an app that helps people find their next pets. We show you listings of all types of pets, mostly dogs, in your area (state or province). We have listings from individual sellers, breeders, or shelters nearby. You can also find cats, reptiles, fish, small furry pets, birds, and toys, accessories, and equipment, for your pets as well.

Barkly has recently been launched in the Apple App Store & Google Play Store.

Languages & Tech

dart, javascript, firebase, google services, html/css

What I did

ux/ui design, mobile app development for iOS & Android devices, website design & development

Project Status

02. Languages

I wanted to make this app as quick as possible, and since I don't know native languagues for mobile development, I knew it would come down to React Native or Flutter.

I have had experience with React Native in the past, and because of that I decided learn Flutter.

03. Learning

I paid $15 for an online Flutter/Dart course. I followed along, and after a week, had some building blocks on how to build the app.

04. Designing

The app is entirely centered around pets (buying, selling, adopting, services), so I wanted to have some colour. I like to keep things really simple, so by default the app navigation background is white. Mixing colour with my simple design allowed me to use a variety(6) of different colours.

06. Features

The few features we have allow users to use our platform in a variety of ways. If they don't have platforms of their own, they can use our app to display contact info, and promote their brands.

07. Backend

Firebase for realtime database, cloud storage, authentication, messaging, and notifications. Firebase makes it really easy to load and update data right away.

08. Messaging

Since I used Firebase, creating an in-app chat wasn't really difficult.

The backend allows the conversations to flow smoothly since the database updates the documents in real time.
