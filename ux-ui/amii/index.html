Amii

Desc. Mobile Socializing App
Role UX/UI Designer & App Developer
Used Dart, Flutter for Android and iOS mobile app





Amii is a socializing app I have been working on for the last few months. Users create posts that are basically jobs they'd do for other people. They aren't regular jobs though, more like socializing jobs.

The app categories are Arts & Culture, Live Shows, Sporting Events, Dining & Nightlife, Outdoor Adventures, and Miscellaneous.

An example of what the app would look like in action. A user creates a post under sporting events. They name a meetup location, add photos, write a description, and set a price. This ad could be anything from watching sports together at a restaurant, going to a live game, or even joining a team together. The possibilities are endless!

There are just some small details that need to be fixed, and the app will be ready for user testing.
